Aggregation – Harmonising Diverse Perspectives

At Cassi, we apply the insight's from Tetlock's Superforecasting, the idea that the median forecast generally outperforms individual forecasts - not just in guessing the number of red marbles in a jar, or, from Francis Galton, the weight of Ox at fair, but across domains. We merge insights from your internal, external, and AI-identified factors and forecasts into a dynamic, evolving analysis of your chances of success. Our aggregation process provides a comprehensive view of the changing probabilities that shape your strategy, ensuring decisions are always informed by the latest information, and allowing you to ask 'what if' questions to refine your approach.
